Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

1. Information We Collect

Cavendish Hostel collects information necessary for the admission and management of hostel residents. This includes personal identification details (Name, Roll Number, Department), contact information, and guardian details.

2. Use of Information

The information collected is used solely for:

  • Processing hostel admission applications.
  • Maintaining residency records and attendance.
  • Communication regarding fees, notices, and emergencies.
  • Ensuring campus security and student welfare.

3. Data Protection

We are committed to ensuring that your information is secure. In order to prevent unauthorized access or disclosure, we have put in place suitable physical, electronic, and managerial procedures to safeguard and secure the information we collect.

4. Third-Party Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your Personally Identifiable Information to outside parties unless we provide users with advance notice. This does not include website hosting partners and other parties who assist us in operating our website, conducting our business, or serving our users, so long as those parties agree to keep this information confidential.

5. Contact Us

If there are any questions regarding this privacy policy, you may contact the Warden at the administrative office.
