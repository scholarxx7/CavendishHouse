Our Legacy

Distinguished Alumni

Patna Science College has been the cradle of brilliance for nearly a century. We take pride in our alumni who have excelled in science, politics, administration, and arts.

VS

Dr. Vashishtha Narayan Singh

Mathematician

A legendary mathematician who challenged Einstein's theory of relativity. His genius was recognized globally, and he remains an icon of pure intelligence born from Bihar.

HV

Prof. H. C. Verma

Physicist & Educator

Renowned for his masterpiece 'Concepts of Physics', he has shaped the understanding of physics for generations of Indian students. A professor at IIT Kanpur.

PK

Prashant Kishor

Political Strategist

Founder of Jan Suraaj. Known for revolutionizing election campaigning in India and working with major political parties across the spectrum.

RG

Rajiv Gauba

Cabinet Secretary

A 1982 batch IAS officer, he served as the Cabinet Secretary of India, the highest executive official in the Government of India.

AN

Abhayanand

IPS Officer & Educator

Former DGP of Bihar and co-founder of Super 30. He is dedicated to coaching underprivileged students for IIT-JEE through 'Abhayanand Super 30'.

SS

Shatrughan Sinha

Actor & Politician

A Bollywood veteran and former Union Minister. Known as 'Bihari Babu', he has had a distinguished career in both cinema and public service.

SM

Sushil Kumar Modi

Politician

Former Deputy Chief Minister of Bihar and a senior leader who played a key role in the state's politics for decades.

TT

Tathagat Avatar Tulsi

Physicist

A child prodigy who completed high school at 9, B.Sc. at 10, and Ph.D. at 21. He is known for his work in quantum physics.

RS

Ravindra Kumar Sinha

Environmentalist

Known as the "Dolphin Man of India" for his conservation efforts for the Gangetic Dolphin. Former Vice-Chancellor of Nalanda Open University.

SD

Sunil Dutt

Former Actor and Politician

A legendary figure in Indian cinema who later served the nation as a Minister of Youth Affairs and Sports. He was a student of Patna College.

AR

Anshul Raj

Advocate

A distinguished legal professional advocating for justice and upholding the values instilled during his time at the institution.
