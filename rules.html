Rules & Regulations

All residents of Cavendish House are expected to maintain high standards of discipline and conduct. Failure to comply with the rules may lead to expulsion.

1. Anti-Ragging Policy

Ragging in any form is strictly prohibited. It is a criminal offense under the law. Any student found guilty of ragging will be immediately expelled and reported to the police.

2. Code of Conduct

  • Students must maintain silence during study hours (7 PM - 10 PM).
  • Respect for fellow boarders and hostel staff is mandatory.
  • Consumption of alcohol, drugs, or any intoxicants is strictly prohibited.
  • Damage to hostel property will result in fines and recovery of costs.

3. Attendance & Leaves

  • Daily attendance will be taken at 9:00 PM. No student is allowed to leave the hostel after this time without prior permission.
  • Students wishing to leave the station must obtain written permission from the Warden and submit a leave application signed by their local guardian.

4. Visitors

  • Male visitors are allowed only in the common room between 4 PM and 7 PM.
  • Female visitors are not permitted in the hostel rooms.
  • No unauthorized person is allowed to stay overnight in the hostel.

5. Mess Rules

  • All residents must join the hostel mess. Cooking in rooms is strictly prohibited.
  • Mess dues must be cleared by the 10th of every month.

By accepting admission to Cavendish Hostel, the student agrees to abide by all the above rules and regulations.
