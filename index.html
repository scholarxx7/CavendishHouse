Our Heritage

A Legacy of Scientific Temper

Named after the illustrious scientist Henry Cavendish, our hostel is an integral part of the historic Patna Science College campus, which was inaugurated by the Viceroy of India, Lord Irwin, on November 15, 1928. For nearly a century, Cavendish House has served as a second home to some of Bihar's most brilliant scientific minds.

We are located at Krishna Ghat, offering a tranquil environment conducive to deep study, just steps away from the holy River Ganga and the college's main administrative block.

Merit-Based Community

Cavendish House is renowned for hosting high-ranking students. Admission is fiercely competitive, often housing the top merit holders immediately following the Faraday House allocation.

How to Apply

Admission is primarily for male students of Science streams (B.Sc).

1

Check Eligibility

You must be an enrolled student of Patna Science College. Allocation is strictly based on Merit Lists from your Secondary/Intermediate exams.

2

Submit Application

Collect the hostel application form from the Principal's Office. Attach your mark sheets and proof of admission.

3

Interview & Allotment

Shortlisted candidates may face a brief interview. Extra weightage is given for NCC, Sports, and Cultural activities.

Note for Girls: Female students of Patna Science College should apply to the G.D.S. Girls Hostel nearby.
