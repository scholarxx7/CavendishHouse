Cavendish Hostel Details & Information
======================================

History & Heritage
------------------
- **Establishment:** The hostel was inaugurated on November 15, 1928.
- **Inaugurated by:** Lord Irwin (Viceroy of India).
- **Architecture:** Heritage building with a design conducive to academic pursuits.
- **Capacity:** Approximately 110 seats.
- **Room Type:** Predominantly single-seater rooms, ensuring privacy for students.

Facilities & Amenities
----------------------
- **Connectivity:** Free Wi-Fi connectivity in rooms.
- **Utilities:** 24/7 Electricity and Water supply (with purification).
- **Recreation:** Common room with LED TV, indoor games.
- **Sports:** Small playgrounds for cricket/football, badminton courts.
- **Services:** Modern laundry and ironing facilities available.
- **Health:** First-aid and emergency ambulance availability.
- **Environment:** Located near the Ganga bank (Krishna Ghat), offering a serene atmosphere.

Admission & Rules
-----------------
- **Eligibility:** Strictly merit-based (Secondary/Intermediate marks).
- **Criteria:** Academic performance + Co-curriculars (NCC, Sports, Cultural).
- **Allotment:** Annual basis; renewal depends on yearly academic performance.
- **Discipline:** High standards of discipline; strict anti-ragging policy.
